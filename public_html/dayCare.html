At Dogs Grooming we provide the social setting in which your dog will learn proper interaction as part of a healthy dog pack while they build relationships through play in a safe, fun environment. At Dogs Grooming Melbourne, we have gone to great lengths in fitting out our dog daycare centre with four large play areas that come complete with heaps of purpose built play equipment designed especially for dogs. From platforms with multiple ramps to winding tunnels and multi layered boxed to jump on, we also have hanging toys to wrestle with and a truck load of non toxic toys and balls to chase. At nap time out come the real sheepskin rugs to snooze on and when the weather heats up, the dogs even enjoy their own bone shaped pool to splash around in, how cool is that! After all dogs love nothing more than to PLAY!
