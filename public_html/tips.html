A new puppy?

A simple checklist of the things that you will need to get you started on a path of bliss, love and happiness

  • A leash and collar with identification and council registration tag
  • Microchip
  • A kennel / crate
  • Dog bed
  • Separate bowls for food and water
  • Dog food
  • Treats
  • Toys
  • Dog brush, comb, nail clippers, etc.
  • Water
  • Pick up bags and bag holder
  • And lots of love!
