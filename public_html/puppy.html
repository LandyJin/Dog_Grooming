Introduce your puppy to dog grooming ... the gentle way


The Dog Grooming Puppy Introduction Program has been designed to gently introduce your puppy to the sights, sounds and smells of the dog grooming salon.

It is a gentle, three step program, designed to take all stress out of the grooming experience for your pup and create a life long positive experience.

A three stage program will familiarise puppies with the workings and processes in a salon. Ten days after your pup's final vaccination, the program may begin:

Vist 1

Your puppy will receive a bath and full groom out, eye trim, nail clip and bottom trim.

This stage is recommended for puppies to 3 months

Vist 2

During this visit, your puppy will receive a bath plus tidy up of face, feet and tail. It is recommended that this occur within 2-4 weeks of Visit 1

Recommended for puppies to 4 months

Vist 3

Now it is time for a full groom. This includes the bath, groom out and full style clip.

This stage should take place within 4-6 weeks of Visit two and is recommended for puppies to 6 months.
