Bath, dry and groom out


Happy and healthy dogs need to be fed a good diet, given regular exercise and kept well groomed. And effective grooming starts with bathing your dog.

Dog Grooming uses hydrobaths to clean your dog the way hand washing alone can’t – the spray head produces a unique hydro-combing action that massages and stimulates the skin, so not only is it the most effective way of washing your dog but also provides a therapeutic water massage. This helps with blood circulation, relieves tired muscles and has been found to be beneficial for arthritis sufferers and older dogs.
